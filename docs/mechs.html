Mech Walkers

Bipedal combat walkers with jump capabilities, dodge mechanics, and interchangeable weapon systems

CSHoverMech Platform

All mech variants are based on the CSHoverMech class which extends ONSHoverCraft. The platform features jump jets, dodge capabilities, and configurable weapon loadouts. Each variant specializes in different combat roles through its unique weapon system.
